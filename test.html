About

Cranium was formed to facilitate the convergence of legacy (Web 2.0) and future (Web 3.0) – critical to the continuing viability of one and the wide-scale adoption of the other.

Nowhere is this more evident than in Financial Services, Payments and Currencies, where parallel universes are evolving, but convergence is either slow or gridlocked.

Legacy Web 2.0 technologies in Banks, Processors, etc. are large, complex and require urgent modernisation.

Newer Web 3.0 technologies used in Fintech, Paytech and Crypto are not designed to operate with the same speed, security, compliance or connectivity of the Web 2.0 domains.

At this point in time, like the coexistence of fossil fuel and renewable energy, there are points of convergence and points of friction. Legacy must adapt or become irrelevant. Meanwhile the new must converge or also become irrelevant.

Nowhere is this more evident than in financial services, payments and currencies, where parallel universes are evolving, but convergence is either slow or gridlocked.

Cranium has developed a unique micro-services transactional orchestration framework, able to support the integration and interoperation of Web 2.0 and Web 3.0 domains, optimized for Payments, Fintech and Crypto – backward and forward compatibility.

Cranium is being used to replace legacy technologies in Payments, where 75% of consumer payments worldwide are processed on technologies from the 1970s and 1980s.

Cranium’s Synap platform aims to become the foundation architecture of multiple new business models, including Payment-as-a-Service and Fintech-as-a-Service models - together these technologies and models are designed to create a profound new relationship between sustainability and payments.
