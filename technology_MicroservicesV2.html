A New Innovative Architecture Built From Scratch:

SYNAP acts as a platform which is capable of executing arbitrary rules: SYNAP self organizes to meet each request. A “rule” is a combination of many things. These include database parameters, dynamic scripts, loadable engines, adaptable message structures and other concepts which enable SYNAP to offer the flexibility and robustness expected from a commercially viable system.

SYNAP is a framework rather than a product or system, which enables creative solutions to problems which are limited only by the richness of the framework's language. A successful framework understands the requirements of its domain without imposing pre-set rules and conditions on how to approach the domain specific solutions.

SYNAP is a deconstructed transactional framework and platform meaning SYNAP can adapt to nearly any future or present demand. If the “kernel” cannot meet the request, the failing component is rewritten and replaced.

enables rapid, robust response to an ever-changing environment. The amount of change required to launch a rapid and robust response will rely on the introduction of a small number of new engines. Deconstructed applications, through micro engine technology, confer a competitive advantage.

a proprietary innovation developed by Cranium to address the problem of internal data representation. Most transactional processing platforms represent information in a fixed format. When this format needs to change, the entire business faces uncertainty. SYNAP's DMC has resolved this issue by representing data internally in an abstract manner that shields an application from changes to the physical structure of the information.

SYNAP has been developed to allow the configuration service to dynamically extend itself so that the “language” used to configure a system remains dynamic, alive and responsive.
